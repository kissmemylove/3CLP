• 3CLP is a random forest model for predicting the cleavage sites of the coronavirus 3CL protease.
  • The input of 3CLP is the protein sequence of either coronavirus polyprotein, or the host proteins; the output is the potential cleavage sites and the corresponding probability predicted by a random forest model
