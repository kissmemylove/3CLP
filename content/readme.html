1.How to obtain the cleavage sites of coronavirus 3CL protease?

Please input the protein sequence in Fasta format(Your protein name should not contain *) in our Home interface.Please ensure your protein sequence contains Q(Glutamine),otherwise your protein sequence cannot find valid predicted sites.

2.How to build the RF model of predicting the cleavage sites?

The RF model trid all combination of AA indexes.Finally,model used the AA indexes of MEEJ800102, BIOV880102 and FASG760101, which referred to “the retention coefficient in high-pressure liquid chromatography”, “Information value for accessibility” and “Molecular weight”, respectively as features. The RF model performed great performance(AUC=0.961).Our training data for 3CLP was available for downloading in Data interface.

3.How to obtain the cleavage sites on human proteins?

You can query the cleavage sites on human proteins in Data interface.1827 potential cleavage sites on human proteins in the table.
